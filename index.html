About Company

Candwan Consultancy Company Limited is a registered firm in Architectural and Quantity Surveying in Tanzania, established in 2025 as limited by shares. Since establishment, the practice has gradually grown with a view of becoming one of the most organized and high profile Consulting group in Built and Construction Industry in the country.

Our Mission

Our mission is focused on qualitative management approach formed to all works that come across; we have experienced consulting firms in the relevant field in order to gain the exposure and expertise through joint venture.

Our Vision

Our practice has so far been successful in the provision of both Quantity surveying and architectural services over the quality of our existing and architectural spectrum of clients (Private-Individuals and a full companies, Institutional e.t.c) in all these, the clients are already enjoying good professional relationship with us and tendered appreciated the professional service rendered.
